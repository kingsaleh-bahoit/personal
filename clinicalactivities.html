Clinical Activities & Practice

Dr. Valentin NKUBA has over 15 years of clinical experience, specializing in infectious diseases, internal medicine, and public health interventions. His patient-centered approach combines cutting-edge medical knowledge with compassionate care to address complex health challenges.

View Consultation Schedule

Clinical Specialties

Infectious Disease Management

Specialized expertise in diagnosing and treating complex infectious diseases, including HIV/AIDS, tuberculosis, malaria, and emerging infectious diseases affecting tropical regions.

Preventive Medicine

Comprehensive preventive care services focusing on early detection, risk assessment, and preventive strategies to optimize health outcomes for individuals and communities.

Internal Medicine

General internal medicine practice providing diagnosis and treatment for adult patients with various health conditions, chronic disease management, and comprehensive health assessments.

Tropical Medicine

Expertise in diagnosing and treating tropical diseases and health conditions specific to tropical regions, with a focus on emerging pathogens and neglected tropical diseases.
