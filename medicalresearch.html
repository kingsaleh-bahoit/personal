Current Research Projects

Tropical Disease Surveillance Network
Ongoing
2023 - Present
Funded by: World Health Organization

Establishing a comprehensive surveillance network for early detection and response to tropical disease outbreaks across five African countries. This project employs innovative digital health technologies and community engagement strategies.

Collaborators: University of Kinshasa, London School of Hygiene & Tropical Medicine, CDC Africa

Maternal and Child Health Intervention Study
Ongoing
2022 - Present
Funded by: Gates Foundation

Evaluating the effectiveness of an integrated package of interventions aimed at reducing maternal and child mortality rates in rural settings. The study combines nutritional support, preventive healthcare services, and educational initiatives.

Collaborators: Ministry of Health, Johns Hopkins University, UNICEF

Antibiotic Resistance Patterns in Community Settings
Completed
2021 - 2023
Funded by: National Institutes of Health

Investigated the emergence and spread of antibiotic-resistant bacteria in community settings, with a focus on identifying contributing factors and developing mitigation strategies. The research involved laboratory analysis, clinical observations, and community surveys.

Collaborators: Institut Pasteur, University of California, San Francisco
