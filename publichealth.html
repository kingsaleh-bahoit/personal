Public Health Programs

Current Projects

Strengthening Healthcare Systems in Rural Africa

Status: Active 2023-2025

A comprehensive program focused on strengthening healthcare infrastructure, training healthcare workers, and improving medical supply chains in rural communities across Africa.

Key Objectives:

  • Train 500+ community health workers
  • Establish sustainable medical supply chains in 20 rural clinics
  • Reduce maternal mortality by 25% in target communities
  • Implement telemedicine solutions to connect rural clinics with specialist care

COVID-19 Pandemic Response & Recovery

Status: Active 2022-2025

Developing sustainable strategies for pandemic recovery and building resilience against future health emergencies in vulnerable communities.

Key Objectives:

  • Establish early warning systems for disease surveillance
  • Create community-based health emergency response teams
  • Develop policy frameworks for pandemic preparedness
  • Conduct research on long-term health impacts of COVID-19

Urban Health Initiative

Status: Planning 2025-2027

A new initiative addressing growing public health challenges in rapidly urbanizing areas, with focus on environmental health, non-communicable diseases, and mental health.

Key Objectives:

  • Develop city-level health strategies for 5 major urban centers
  • Create accessible mental health services in underserved urban communities
  • Implement air quality monitoring and mitigation programs
  • Design effective interventions for non-communicable disease prevention
